Walasis Gold Candle Sconces Wall Decor

Elevate your home's aesthetic with the timeless elegance of the Walasis Gold Candle Sconces. These beautifully designed wall sconces bring a touch of vintage French charm, merging classic style with modern functionality. Their arched shape paired with mirror elements creates a warm and inviting glow, perfect for adding a sophisticated ambiance to your living space.

Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your bedroom, living room, or entryway, these sconces are versatile and easy to integrate into any interior decor. The detachable bow adds a customizable touch, allowing you to match your style or create a seasonal look for holidays like Christmas and Halloween. Made with high-quality iron, they are built to last and offer an antique gold finish that exudes old-world beauty and warmth.

  • Classic French design with mirror accents that enhance light reflection
  • Durable iron construction ensuring long-lasting use
  • Versatile tray for taper, pillar, and flameless candles
  • Detachable cast iron bow for easy customization
  • Antique gold finish adds timeless beauty and warmth to any room
