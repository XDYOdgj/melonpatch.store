IZIDDO Candle Warmer Lamp with Timer and Dimmer

Safe and Clean Operation

Unlike traditional candles, the IZIDDO Candle Warmer Lamp features a 50-watt halogen bulb that warms wax gently from the top down. This eliminates the dangers of fire and the mess of soot, offering a safe and decorative alternative for your home.

With a dimmable light function, you can adjust the intensity to suit your mood and control the melting speed for a more tailored experience. The adjustable timer allows for 2, 4, or 8-hour settings, so you can set your lamp to work at your convenience.
