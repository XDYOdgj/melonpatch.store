Elegant Wooden Wall Sconce with Removable Glass Cylinder

The Shae Transitional Wall Sconce from Kate and Laurel is a seamless blend of rustic charm and modern elegance. Designed to bring warmth and texture to your space, its natural wood construction adds a comforting, organic feel that's perfect for any room.

With a minimal design approach, this sconce offers a timeless aesthetic that complements a wide range of interior styles. The removable glass cylinder is a standout feature, allowing you to switch between a classic, warm glow and a contemporary, artistic look. You can display flameless candles or simply leave it open for a clean, modern statement.

Whether you're looking to enhance the ambiance of your bedroom or create a decorative focal point in your bathroom, this sconce fits effortlessly into any setting. The 5x5x13 dimensions ensure a strong visual presence without overwhelming the space, making it an ideal choice for flanking mirrors or beds.

Its easy-to-install design, complete with an attached keyhole hanger, ensures a hassle-free setup. This sconce is not just a light fixture; it's a stylish addition to your home that combines function and beauty in perfect harmony.
